Perfect Lemonade Recipe

Home Recipe Selling Tips

Ingredients:

  • 6 lemons
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 6 cups water
  • Ice

Steps:

  1. Squeeze lemons to get 1 cup of juice.
  2. Mix sugar with 1 cup warm water until dissolved.
  3. Combine lemon juice, sugar water, and remaining water.
  4. Chill and serve over ice!
